Lash Sets

Explore our range of eyelash sets, from barely noticeable to the most dramatic effects.

Classic Set (1D)

The most natural form of eyelash extension, where a single lash extension is applied to each natural lash. Ideal if you prefer a natural effect.

  • **Effect:** Natural extension, mascara look
  • **Number of Lashes:** 1 extension per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those trying extensions for the first time or desiring the most natural result.

2D Set (Hybrid / Light Volume)

The most natural Volume technique, where two thin Volume fans are applied to each natural lash. Ideal for those wanting a subtle enhancement.

  • **Effect:** Natural, subtle
  • **Number of Lashes:** 2 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those trying Volume sets for the first time or wanting a natural yet fuller effect.

3-7D Volume Set

A fuller, denser lash line. Ideal for achieving a more dramatic look, perfect even for thin, sparse natural lashes, as the Volume fans cover the sparser areas.

  • **Effect:** Dramatic, dense, Volume
  • **Number of Lashes:** 3-7 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those already experienced with Volume sets or who desire a dense, noticeable lash line.

8-10D Mega Volume Set

The most intense, densest effect, maximizing drama and fullness. Fans made from extremely thin lashes ensure a weightless and long-lasting wear.

  • **Effect:** Extreme, Ultra Volume, most intense density
  • **Number of Lashes:** 8-10 (or more) Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Fans of extreme makeup, performers, or those requiring maximum fullness.

    **It is crucial** to clean your lashes daily or every other day with a specialized lash shampoo (available for purchase from us). Clean lashes are not only hygienic but also ensure better adhesive bonding during refills.

  • Oil-Free Products

    All oil-based cosmetics and makeup removers should be avoided around the eye area. Oil dissolves the adhesive, leading to premature lash loss.

  • Avoid Rubbing or Picking

    Rubbing or picking your lashes can severely damage your natural lashes and inevitably results in much earlier refills. Only comb through your lashes with a clean spoolie brush.

These simple steps ensure that your eyelash extensions remain perfect for the longest possible time.
