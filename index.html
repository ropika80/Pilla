Lash Set Styles

Explore our selection of lash sets, from the barely noticeable to the most dramatic effect.

Classic Set (1D)

The most natural form of eyelash extension, where a single lash extension is applied to each natural lash. Ideal if you prefer a natural effect.

  • **Effect:** Natural extension, mascara effect
  • **Number of Lashes:** 1 extension per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those who want to extend and thicken their natural lashes without a noticeable look.

2D Set (Doubling)

In the 2D technique, two thin Volume fans are applied to each natural lash. Subtle fullness and a mascara effect ‚Äì the perfect transition between natural and denser looks.

  • **Effect:** Subtle, elegant fullness
  • **Number of Lashes:** 2 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those who want a fuller lash line but don't want heavy makeup.

3-4D Set (Light Volume)

The entry level of Volume techniques. 3-4 ultra-light lashes are applied to each natural lash. Results in noticeable but not overly dramatic fullness.

  • **Effect:** Denser lash line, emphasized look
  • **Number of Lashes:** 3-4 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those switching from a Classic set who still prefer a lighter look.

5-7D Volume Set

With this technique, 5-7 light but light Volume fans are applied to the natural lashes. Results in stunning fullness and a dramatic lash line.

  • **Effect:** Dramatic, striking fullness
  • **Number of Lashes:** 5-7 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those who want the fullest, most noticeable lash line.

8-10D Mega Volume Set

The technique providing the greatest fullness, where up to 8-10 extremely thin and lightweight lashes are applied to each natural lash. Maximum impact and a dark lash line.

  • **Effect:** Maximum fullness, "black line" effect
  • **Number of Lashes:** 8-10 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those desiring the boldest, most luxurious appearance.

Eyelash Aftercare Guide

Follow these steps to ensure perfect retention and hygiene.

  • The 48-Hour Rule

    For **48 hours** after application, avoid direct contact with water, steam, sauna, and high humidity. The adhesive needs this time to fully cure.

  • Daily Cleaning and Hygiene

    It is **essential** to clean your lashes daily or every other day with a special eyelash shampoo (which can be purchased from us). Clean lashes are not only hygienic, but the glue also adheres better during refills.

  • Oil-Free Products

    All oil-containing cosmetics and makeup removers must be avoided around the eyes. Oil dissolves the adhesive, leading to premature lash loss.

  • Brush and Brushing

    Gently brush your lashes twice a day, from root to tip, with the special eyelash brush provided by us. This helps maintain the shape.

  • Avoid Rubbing and Pulling

    Never rub your eyes! Pulling at your extensions can cause permanent damage to your natural lashes. Always handle your lashes gently.

IMPORTANT: Improper home removal can damage your natural lashes. If you wish to have your extensions removed, please contact me.
