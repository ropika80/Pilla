Lash Sets

Explore our range of eyelash sets, from barely noticeable to the most dramatic effects.

Classic Set (1D)

The most natural form of eyelash extension, where a single lash extension is applied to each natural lash. Ideal if you prefer a natural effect.

  • **Effect:** Natural extension, mascara look
  • **Number of Lashes:** 1 extension per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those trying extensions for the first time or desiring the most natural result.

2D Set (Hybrid / Light Volume)

The most natural Volume technique, where two thin Volume fans are applied to each natural lash. Ideal for those who want a subtle enhancement.

  • **Effect:** Natural, subtle
  • **Number of Lashes:** 2 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those trying Volume sets for the first time, or desiring a natural but fuller look.

3-4D Set (Volume)

Our most popular Volume set, creating a perfect balance between noticeable fullness and a still natural appearance.

  • **Effect:** Noticeable fullness, yet lightweight wear
  • **Number of Lashes:** 3-4 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those who want to accentuate their eyes without an overly dramatic result.

5-7D Volume Set (Volume)

Extreme volume technique, where dense but light Volume fans are applied to the natural lashes. Results in stunning fullness and a dramatic lash line.

  • **Effect:** Dramatic, striking fullness
  • **Number of Lashes:** 5-7 Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Those already experienced with Volume sets or desiring a dense, noticeable lash line.

8-10D Mega Volume Set

The most intense, densest effect, maximizing drama and fullness. Fans made from extremely thin lashes ensure a weightless and long-lasting wear.

  • **Effect:** Extreme, Ultra Volume, most intense density
  • **Number of Lashes:** 8-10 (or more) Volume fans per natural lash
  • **Recommended for:** Fans of extreme makeup, performers, or those requiring maximum fullness.

Eyelash Aftercare Guide

Follow these steps for perfect retention and hygiene.

  • The 48-Hour Rule

    For **48 hours** after application, avoid direct contact with water, steam, saunas, and high humidity. The adhesive needs this time to fully cure.

  • Daily Cleaning and Hygiene

    **It is crucial** to clean your lashes daily or every other day with a specialized lash shampoo (available for purchase from us). Clean lashes are not only hygienic, but the adhesive also adheres better during refills.

  • Oil-Free Products

    Avoid all oil-containing cosmetics and makeup removers around the eyes. Oil dissolves the adhesive, leading to premature lash loss.

  • Avoid Rubbing and Picking

    Rubbing or picking your lashes can severely damage your natural eyelashes and will inevitably lead to much earlier refills. Only comb your lashes with a clean spoolie brush.

These simple steps ensure your eyelash extensions remain perfect for the longest possible time.
